Online Courses

Consumption activities are interpreted as an activity carried out by humans to spend the selling value of goods and services while at the same time fulfilling their needs. The party that carries out this activity is known as the consumer. In practice, consumers can be individuals or groups. The goal also varies, depending on the needs of each consumer. Individuals who carry out these activities aim to fulfill their personal needs. Some of the activities carried out can also be intended to please or satisfy yourself. A motorcyclist who buys gas or a woman who orders a package of spa services are some of the consumption activities carried out individually

The activity of buying, using, and spending all kinds of goods and services is called a consumption activity if it fulfills the following characteristics: Done directly with the aim of satisfying a want or meeting a current need. The goods purchased or services used provide value for the benefit of those who carry out these activities. Sooner or later, the utility value of the goods and services consumed will decrease or be exhausted.
